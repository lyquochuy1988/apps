How can you keep your data safe?

The primary rule of keeping data safe is to never never never store it anywhere online.

It does not matter how many neon lights the server has and how many 'armed' guards the server location has, and how many layers of antihacker protection the server has, the fact of the matter is that all kinds of supposedly secure sites are being successfully attacked.

Keep the solution simple !

The most simple solutions are often the best. And that being said, nothing beats the security of writing down your logins and passwords in a good old 99 cent notebook from walmart and keeping it in your safe.

However this is not always practical, for example when you are travelling or you are at work and you need some information.

What is LockDiary?

LockDiary is an Armored Digital Data Keeper that allows you to file away massive amounts of data, and you get to file each one the old fashioned way, into an alphabetized database. The app shows you 27 Rolodex card categories, from A to Z. Depending on the first letter of your info, it is filed into these cards. The app gives you the convenience of the good old alphabetized phone book, with the modern convenience of being able to hold any number of data entries, along with powerful search functionality.

Powerful Encryption

Every time you close and exit the app, the data is encypted securely. And it stays that way until you open the app again and enter your password. But a word of caution: there is no password recovery feature, so if you forget your password, you can say bye bye to the data stored in LockDiary. We have zero access to your phone, and we want to keep it that way. We cannot and will not assist with password recovery. So, make it a password that you can remember without having to write it down.
